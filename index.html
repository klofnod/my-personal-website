This is a place to learn a little bit about me

I have spent most of my life as a mechanic, I have always enjoyed fixing problems and diagnosing issues. The more I was in mechanics though the more my passion for learing more complex systems evolved. Vehicle Computers are an area that lots of mechanics stuggle with and I had my fair share of hard to solve issues with them. That drove me to learn more about the computers and systems in place within the vehicle manufacturing world, which in turn lead me to coding and its prevalence in our every day life. The growth of the information sector and the wide variety of potential jobs have me very interested in joining this sector for my career.
